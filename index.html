ABOUT US.

We are a team of people who in 2022 sat down and decided to create an environment to nurture athletes.

We aim to manage Sports events, be it 15s, 10s or 7s for rugby, or tournaments and scrimmages for basketball.

We also aim to provide sports coaches to schools to further extend our long term goals of developing and improving sports in Uganda.
